About

2022-07-30 :: ijrussell

My name is Ian and I’m a software developer with over 25 years of experience, most of it in the .NET (Core) space. I have been using C# since 2003 and F# since 2012. I have been a regular presenter at .NET User Groups and community conferences around the UK, mainly talking about F#, functional programming, and Domain-Driven Design.

I’m the author of a free ebook, Essential F#, which you can download from https://leanpub.com/essential-fsharp.

The source markdown files are available at https://github.com/ijrussell/essential-fsharp.

I’m currently learning golang.
